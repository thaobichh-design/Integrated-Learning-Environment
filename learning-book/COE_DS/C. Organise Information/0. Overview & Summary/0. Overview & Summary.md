| **SUBJECT CONTENT** | Effective in Effective Data Science = **Sustainable, Efficient, and Scalable *data science*.** Sustainable Data Science: your *data science* work keeps working over time (reproducible pipelines, documented assumptions, controlled technical debt). Efficient Data Science: you get the most *data science* impact for the effort (right problem framing, validation, communication). Scalable Data Science: your *data science* practice can grow (reusable code, shared methods, captured knowledge). So: Effective Data Science = Data Science that is Sustainable, Efficient, and Scalable—applied to turning data into reliable, actionable decisions. | Sustainable: data science that isn't sustainable breaks when people or context change; Effective Data Science stays reliable so your *data science* impact lasts. Efficient: ineffective data science burns time on redo’s and re-explaining; Effective Data Science focuses effort where it changes decisions for maximum impact per unit of effort. Scalable: ineffective data science stays stuck at one project or one person; Effective Data Science can grow so your *data science* influence multiplies. So: being Effective in Data Science makes your work lasting (Sustainable), high-leverage (Efficient), and able to grow (Scalable)—turning data into decisions that stick, at the right cost, and at scale. |

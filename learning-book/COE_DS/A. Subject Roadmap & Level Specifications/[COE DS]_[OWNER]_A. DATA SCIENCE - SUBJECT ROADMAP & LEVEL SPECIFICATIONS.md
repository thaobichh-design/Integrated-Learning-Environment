## Data Science: Subject-Specific Requirements & Credentials

*Populate per level. Use Hierarchy of Science when defining competencies.*

### L1 – Follow

- **Requirements:**
  1. Runs basic data loads and simple queries under direction.
  2. Learns Python/R fundamentals, SQL SELECT, basic descriptive stats.
  3. Follows data-prep scripts and Jupyter notebooks.

- **Credentials:**
  1. Introduction to Data Science (Coursera, DataCamp, edX).
  2. Python/R basics; SQL fundamentals.
  3. Kaggle Learn or equivalent.

### L2 – Assist

- **Requirements:**
  1. Builds ETL pipelines with guidance; cleans and validates data.
  2. Creates dashboards in Tableau/Power BI; supports analysts with ad hoc queries.
  3. Documents data dictionaries and lineage.

- **Credentials:**
  1. Google Data Analytics / IBM Data Science Professional Certificate.
  2. SQL, Python, basic stats and viz.
  3. Portfolio: 2–3 end-to-end projects.

### L3 – Apply

- **Requirements:**
  1. Designs and deploys models (e.g. regression, classification) in production.
  2. Performs A/B tests, cohort analysis, and attribution.
  3. Produces reproducible analysis with version control (git, DVC).

- **Credentials:**
  1. AWS/Google Cloud Data Engineer or Data Scientist certification.
  2. Kaggle competitions or equivalent portfolio.
  3. 1–2 years applied experience.

### L4 – Enable

- **Requirements:**
  1. Leads data science projects; mentors junior analysts.
  2. Implements MLOps, model monitoring, and feature stores.
  3. Defines standards for experimentation, documentation, and reproducibility.

- **Credentials:**
  1. Advanced degree (MS Data Science) or equivalent depth.
  2. 3+ years applied experience; mentorship evidence.
  3. Published work or open-source contributions.

### L5 – Ensure / Advise

- **Requirements:**
  1. Designs org-wide analytics and ML architecture.
  2. Advises on data strategy, governance, and AI ethics.
  3. Leads tooling, platform, and talent decisions for data teams.

- **Credentials:**
  1. PhD or specialist Masters in quantitative field.
  2. 5+ years; leadership of teams.
  3. Thought leadership (talks, articles, standards).

### L6 – Initiate / Influence

- **Requirements:**
  1. Sets enterprise data & AI strategy; allocates budget and headcount.
  2. Influences C-suite on AI/analytics investments and risk.
  3. Owns data governance, privacy, and ethics frameworks.

- **Credentials:**
  1. Head of Data / Chief Data Officer or equivalent.
  2. 8+ years; executive exposure.
  3. Board or advisory experience.

### L7 – Set Strategy / Inspire / Mobilize

- **Requirements:**
  1. As CDO/CTO, defines global data & AI vision; mobilizes funding for AI transformation.
  2. Champions responsible AI, ethics, and governance at board level.
  3. Steers data-driven M&A, partnerships, and platform strategy.

- **Credentials:**
  1. C-suite (CDO, CTO, CPO).
  2. Board-level stewardship.
  3. Public/listed company or equivalent scale.

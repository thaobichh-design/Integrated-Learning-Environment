## Perspective Rule
> *All questions in cols 3–14 are answered from the **row subject's own perspective**. Row subject = the component. "Success" = component is installed, configured, and functioning as designed for Topic 1 (UBS). "Failure" = component is absent, misconfigured, or misused for this Topic — regardless of whether it works elsewhere.*

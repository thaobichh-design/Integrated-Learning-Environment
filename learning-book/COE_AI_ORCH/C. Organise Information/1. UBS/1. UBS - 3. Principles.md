## Note on P1–P5 as one causal chain

P1–P5 are not five independent rules — they operate as one causal sequence applied to every build cycle. P1 (minimum scope) makes the first build achievable. P2 (testable artefact + A.C.) establishes what a valid cycle output looks like. P4 (A.C. written before Cursor opens) keeps scope bounded during the build. P3 (extract one principle) converts the clean outcome into composable knowledge. P5 (ELF micro-cycle Measure step) ensures that extraction is structured and precise. Together they form the mechanism that closes the Capability Gap one cycle at a time. P6 and P7 name the risky beliefs that disable this chain from the inside — recognising them is the pre-condition for applying P1–P5 reliably.

# Topic 1. UBS — Page 5: Steps to Apply
*Phase C. Organise Information | Topic: 1. UBS | Page: 5. Steps to Apply*
*Subject: AI Orchestration / Engineering | UDO: Build and master an OE System that any analyst/operator can use to build domain-specific UE systems. Effective = Sustainable, Efficient, Scalable.*

## Perspective Rule
> *All questions in cols 3–14 are answered from the **row subject's own perspective**. Row subject = the step. "Success" = step is completed as designed and its gate is passed. "Failure" = step is skipped, done out of order, or gate not met.*

## Gate Logic
Each step has an explicit gate. The gate must be passed before the next step begins. Advancing without meeting the gate violates P2 (A.C. principle) and breaks the Incremental Build-Test Cycle.
